01 About us

Our formation and development journeyOur formation and development journey

Vietsafe E&C JSC was established in 2025 as a spin-off of the Consulting & Design division of Vietnam Safety Technology Joint Stock Company (VIETSAFE., JSC).

We inherit the full design engineering team, the methodology, and the proven track record built over 17+ years — from 150m+ high-rises and petrochemical complexes to metro lines and international airports.

As an independent E&C entity, Vietsafe E&C focuses purely on consulting and design — vendor-neutral, performance-based, and fully compliant with current Vietnamese fire safety law.

02 Engineering services

Specialised consulting & design capabilitiesSpecialised consulting & design capabilities

S-01

Fire & Life Safety (FLS) Strategy Consulting

Fire & Life Safety (FLS) strategy

FLS strategy reports for super high-rise & special facilities (airports, metro, oil & gas…): egress, compartmentation, smoke management, structural fire safety.

S-02

Fire protection design

Fire protection system design

Conceptual and detailed engineering of fire alarm, suppression (water/gas/foam), smoke control, and fire-water supply systems per QCVN 06 and TCVN.

S-03

Industrial facility design

Industrial facility consulting & design

Fire protection design for industrial facilities: petroleum, LPG, LNG, chemical and bitumen/asphalt depots and terminals — per NFPA, EN and specialised QCVN/TCVN.

S-04

Alternative Solution & Foreign Code Application

NFPA standard substitution

Preparing code-equivalency matrices (NFPA vs QCVN), technical justifications, and authority approvals for the application of foreign fire codes.

S-05

Design review & acceptance

Design review & acceptance

Reviewing design on behalf of the owner; organising the fire safety self-acceptance dossier in line with current regulations.

S-06

Fire Emergency & Rescue Plan Consulting

Fire & rescue contingency plans

Firefighting, search-and-rescue and emergency response plans for plants, depots and industrial infrastructure.

S-08

Performance-Based Fire Design & Simulation

Fire dynamics simulation (FDS/PyroSim) · Egress modelling (Pathfinder) · ASET/RSET analysis

Fire & smoke simulation (FDS/PyroSim), egress modelling (Pathfinder), technical justification dossiers for projects beyond the QCVN scope — super high-rise, airports, metro, oil & gas.
